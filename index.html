Visit Amritsar

Enjoy culture and Amritsari cuisine in this vibrant district found by Guru Ramdas Ji.

Top three activities to do at Torshov

Golden Temple

Amritsar is world-famous for the beautiful and highly revered Golden Temple or Sri Harmandir Sahib, which is one of the most prominent spiritual sites in the country

Wagah Border

Wagah, an army outpost on the Indo-Pakistan border - between Amritsar and Lahore -- is an elaborate complex of buildings, roads and barriers on both sides.

Ram Tirath

Legendary birthplace of Lav and Kush and the spot where the sage Valmiki's ashram stood. The town has an ancient tank and many temples.
